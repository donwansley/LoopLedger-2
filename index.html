Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, upload songs, or contact us for support. This includes your name, email address, payment information, and music metadata.

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, process transactions, send you updates, and respond to your inquiries.
